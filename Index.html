FAQ

Questions vendors always ask

Is it really free to become a vendor?
Yes — listing products on MC Store is completely free. We only earn when you make a sale, by adding our margin on top of your base price. There are no monthly fees, setup fees or hidden charges.
How long does verification take?
We aim to review and approve all vendor applications within 24 hours. You'll receive an SMS and email notification once your account is approved or if we need additional information.
Who handles delivery to customers?
MC Store handles all deliveries using our logistics partners. Once an order is placed, you prepare and package the item. Our courier collects from your location and delivers to the customer nationwide.
When and how do I get paid?
Earnings are paid directly to your registered bank account within 48 hours of successful order delivery. You can track all your earnings in your vendor dashboard in real-time.
Can I sell any type of product?
Currently MC Store focuses on phone accessories, electronics and related gadgets. All products are reviewed by our team before going live to ensure quality and authenticity.
What if a customer returns a product?
Returns are handled case by case. If the product was faulty or not as described, MC Store covers the return. If it was a customer preference return, the vendor's earnings for that order are reversed. Full policy is in our Vendor Terms.
